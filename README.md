## Research Question
Is exposure to volatile organic compounds associated with blood pressure among nonsmokers?

## Methods
- **Study population:** National Health and Nutrition Examination Survey 2011-2018
- **Exposures:** Urinary metabolites of acrolein, benzene, 1,3-butadiene, crotonaldehyde, and styrene
- **Outcome:** systolic blood pressure, diastolic blood pressure, hypertension
- **Statistical analysis:** survey weighted linear regression, modified Poisson regression, Bayesian kernel machine regression
